## 精选提示词示例

### 写实人物摄影
```
Portrait of a young Chinese woman, professional photography
Style: editorial fashion, soft natural lighting
Details: shallow depth of field, 85mm lens, f/1.8
Mood: confident and elegant
Color palette: warm tones, cream and gold
Quality: 8K, hyper-realistic, professional color grading
--ar 2:3 --v 6.0 --style raw
```

### 赛博朋克城市
```
Cyberpunk cityscape, night scene, Tokyo-inspired
Neon lights reflecting on wet streets
Flying cars in the distance
Holographic advertisements in Japanese and English
Cinematic lighting, volumetric fog
Blade Runner aesthetic, rain
--ar 16:9 --v 6.0 --style raw
```

### 水墨国风插画
```
Traditional Chinese ink wash painting style
Lotus flowers on a misty pond at dawn
Minimalist composition, negative space
Black ink on rice paper texture
Subtle gold accents on petals
Ethereal atmosphere, zen garden mood
--ar 3:4 --v 6.0
```

### 白底电商产品图
```
Product photography of [product name]
White background, studio lighting
Clean and minimalist composition
Soft shadow, professional e-commerce style
Front view and 45-degree angle
8K resolution, commercial product photography
--ar 1:1 --v 6.0 --style raw
```

```
Industry: [tech/fashion/food/health]
Style: modern, clean, geometric
Colors: navy blue and gold
Flat vector, scalable, no text
White background, centered composition
--ar 1:1 --v 6.0
```

## 附赠：提示词工程指南

1. **万能公式**：主体 + 风格 + 构图 + 光影 + 画质 + 比例
2. **权重语法**：用 `::` 控制关键词权重（如 `red::2 blue::1`）
3. **负面提示词清单**（SD/ComfyUI 用户必备）
4. **Midjourney 参数速查卡**（--ar, --chaos, --stylize 等完整说明）
